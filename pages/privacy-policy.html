Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the project management tool Join, which was developed as part of a professional development program.

Developers: Daniel Tran and Lucas Hamacher


1. Collection and Use of Data

We collect and store personal data only to the extent necessary for the use of Join. This includes, among others, name, email address, and phone number.


2. Storage and Security

All stored data is protected with modern security measures to prevent unauthorized access.


3. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose any personal data to third parties unless required by law or necessary for the operation of Join.


4. User Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their data. Requests can be sent by email to the development team.


5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. Changes become effective upon publication on the website.
